I hate to be a nitpicking fanboy, but seriously, they added about twenty extra minutes of their own stuff when they could have been focusing on the important subplots . It seems these days, when it comes to book-to-film adaptation, you can get one or the other of a great film or a faithful film; you can't get both. It's the same here: great film, crap transitional adaptation.
